About
Observing the silent flight of a samara, or winged seed escaping its parents shadow is almost certainly a universal human experience. An encounter with one of Nature's helicopters probably inspired the first manifestation of mankindâ€™s ideas of vertical flight. This most basic example of flight in Nature shed light onto greatly simplified flight control mechanisms and a pattern to increased aerodynamic performance in a Low Reynolds number environment. While many others look to the animal kingdom for solutions implemented by Nature, our mono-wing rotorcraft mimics a solution discovered in the plant kingdom and implemented on a highly maneuverable aircraft, the Roboseed. The advantages of this unique rotorcraft is inspired by winged seeds such as a maple seed, and is patented and exclusively licensed by the Inventor, a founder of Aeroseed.
